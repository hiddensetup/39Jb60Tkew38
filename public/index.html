Welcome to Routin.cloud

How to register?

  1. Go to register and enter your floor number and department letter.
  2. Submit and accept incoming notifications.

You can add all devices you want to receive notifications.

How to check notifications sent?

  1. Go to board page.
  2. Search notifications messages using filters.

Who can send messages?

Anyone with permission to send messages can send messages anonymously (no one will know who sent the message). We don't store personal information because the platform is designed to send notifications to departments.

Can users send messages?

Yes, anyone can send massive notifications to a specific floor, or to a specific floor and department from the sender page without exposing personal data.
